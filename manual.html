User Guide

Everything you need to track your finances, manage accounts, and keep your books clean — on mobile and web.

▶ Download on Google Play
ℹ️

Free for personal useAccount Pro is free to download and use. If you need a feature that's not there yet, reach out — we'll try to build it for you.

Account Pro is a double-entry accounting app designed for individuals, students, and small business owners. It keeps your books accurate, your data private (fully offline), and your workflow smooth — whether you're on a phone or a browser.

Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up Account Pro from scratch.

1 · Create a Company

A "Company" is your accounting workspace. You can have multiple companies (e.g. Personal, Business) and switch between them at any time.

1

Open Account Pro

On first launch you'll be prompted to create your first company.

2

Enter a company name

Use a clear, consistent name (e.g. "My Business 2025"). This name is also used for cloud sync — keep it identical across devices.

3

Choose currency & fiscal year

Set your preferred currency and the start month of your financial year.

4

Tap "Create Company"

Your workspace is ready. You can add more companies later from the sidebar menu.

2 · Add Accounts & Account Groups

Accounts represent financial categories (e.g. Cash, Bank, Sales Revenue). Group them under standard headings: Assets, Liabilities, Income, Expenses, Equity.

💡

TipAccount Pro comes with default account groups. You can add custom sub-groups to match your business structure.

1

Go to Accounts

Tap the Accounts icon in the bottom navigation.

2

Tap the + button

Choose an account group, then enter a name and opening balance.

3

Save the account

It will immediately appear in your chart of accounts.

3 · Add Transactions

Every transaction in Account Pro follows double-entry: every debit has a matching credit. You can use Easy Mode for quick entries, or Journal Entry for complex multi-ledger transactions.

1

Tap + New Entry

Select Easy Mode (two accounts) or Journal Entry (multiple accounts).

2

Select accounts & amount

Choose the debit and credit accounts, enter the amount and date.

3

Add a note or image

Optionally attach a memo or voucher photo for reference.

4

Save

The transaction is recorded and ledgers update instantly.

📝

Journal Entries

Account Pro fully supports standard journal entries, including complex entries involving more than two ledgers in a single voucher.

Simple Entry

One debit, one credit. Use Easy Mode for day-to-day transactions like cash purchases or bank transfers.

Complex Entry

Multiple debits or credits in a single voucher. Great for payroll, tax entries, or splitting purchases across departments.

ℹ️

Performance testedAccount Pro has been tested with over 10,000 entries without slowdowns — you don't need to worry about large datasets.

📊

Ledger & Reports

View the full transaction history for any account, filter by date, and export when you need a paper trail.

1

Open an Account

From the Accounts screen, tap any account to open its ledger.

2

Filter by Date Range

Use the date picker at the top to narrow entries to a specific period.

3

Print or Export as PDF

Tap the share icon and choose Print or Save as PDF. The PDF is formatted cleanly for sharing with accountants or auditors.

Mobile ↔ Web Sync

How It Works

Account Pro uses a server-assisted sync model. Since we keep the app free, we don't run a real-time sync server — instead, the mobile app uploads a compressed database snapshot every 15 minutes (only when new entries exist), and the web version lets you download the latest snapshot on demand.

Setting Up Sync

📱 On Mobile

Go to Settings → Sync and enable Automatic Sync. The app will upload your database every 15 minutes in the background whenever new entries are detected.

🖥️ On Web

Open your company on the web version. Tap Download Latest Data to pull the most recent upload from your phone. Auto-download coming soon.

✅

Check your database sizeIf the database size shows 0.5 KB or less, no records have been uploaded yet. Make sure Automatic Sync is turned on in the mobile app.

💾

Backup & Restore

Your data lives on your device. Use the built-in backup tool to create a local copy you can move to another phone or keep as a safety net.

Create a Backup

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. A .db file will be saved to your chosen folder.

Restore from Backup

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Restore, then select your backup file. Note: this replaces current data.

💡

Enable Auto Backup to have the app create a daily backup automatically. We recommend also copying your backup to Google Drive or another cloud service.

📥

Import & Export

Move data in and out of Account Pro using Excel files — no manual re-entry needed.

1

Export a template

Go to Import/Export → Download Template to get the correct Excel format for accounts or transactions.

2

Fill in your data

Open the template in Excel or Google Sheets and enter your records, keeping the column headers intact.

3

Import the file

Tap Import → Select File and choose your filled Excel file. The app will validate and import your records.

⚠️

Do not rename or reorder columns in the template. The importer depends on the column structure to map data correctly.

🖼️

Voucher Images

Attach a photo of a receipt, bill, or invoice directly to any transaction voucher. Images are stored with your data and visible on both the mobile app and web version when synced.

1

Open or create a transaction

Tap any existing voucher, or create a new one.

2

Tap "Attach Image"

Choose from your gallery or take a photo directly in the app.

3

Sync to see on web

After enabling sync, voucher images are uploaded alongside the database and will appear on the web version.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Account Pro really free?

Yes. Account Pro is free for personal use. Almost all sections of the app are ad-free. Advertisements, where present, are minimal and non-intrusive.

Is my data safe? Does the app send data anywhere?

The app is fully offline by default. Data is only uploaded to the cloud if you explicitly enable Automatic Sync. No data is shared with third parties.

Can I use Account Pro on multiple phones?

Yes. Enable Automatic Sync on all devices and use the same company name. Each device will push its updates to the server, and you can pull the latest data on any device.

My sync shows 0.5 KB. What does that mean?

A very small database file usually means no records have been uploaded yet. Make sure Automatic Sync is turned on in Settings → Sync and that you have at least one entry saved.

Can I use this for my small business?

Account Pro is built on double-entry principles and supports multiple accounts, account groups, journal entries, and ledger printing — all features a small business needs. Reach out if you have specific requirements.

What's coming next?

The roadmap includes complex journal entries for sales & purchases with tax, more financial charts & reports, and budgeting tools. Have a feature request? Let us know!
