Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 20, 2024  ·  Account Pro (Account Sir)  ·  Dev Pandey

🛡️
Your accounting data belongs to you — always. Account Pro does not collect, store, or access any of your financial records, transactions, or account entries. All your data is kept entirely on your device and synced only to your own Google Drive — no third-party server ever sees it.

📋 Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to your use of this app (Account Pro / Account Sir). This app does not represent any government entity and is built solely for personal and business bookkeeping purposes.

This Policy describes the information we collect through the App and how we use it. This app requires access to your device's external storage to backup and restore the database. You can grant or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

✅ Your Accounting Data — Never Collected

We want to be completely transparent: Account Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any of your financial data. Your accounts, transactions, ledger entries, and reports exist only on your device.

When you choose to back up your data, it is saved directly to your personal Google Drive account — encrypted and under your full control. We have no access to it whatsoever.

✅ Account entries stay on device
✅ Backup goes to YOUR Google Drive
✅ No server stores your ledger data
✅ No third party sees your finances
✅ You own your data, always
✅ Revoke backup access anytime

ℹ️ Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • User Account Information: When you sign in with your Google account, we collect your basic profile information — name, profile photo, and email address. This is used solely for account management within the App and is never shared with any third party.
  • Device Information: We collect basic information about your device (model, operating system version, unique device identifier) for technical purposes such as analyzing app performance and troubleshooting issues.

☁️ Google Drive Backup & Sync

Account Pro offers an optional backup feature that saves your database to your own Google Drive. This is the only way your data leaves your device — and it goes directly to your personal storage, not our servers.

  • Only you can access your Drive backup — we cannot.
  • You can delete the backup file from your Google Drive at any time.
  • You can revoke the app's Drive access from your Google account settings.
  • This feature is completely optional. The app works fully offline without it.

You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: google.com/ads/preferences

🎯 Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To manage your account
  • To analyze how you use the App and improve performance
  • To personalize your experience within the App
  • To communicate with you about the App — updates, security alerts, and support
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

🤝 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party, except for Google AdMob as described above. We may share anonymized and aggregated usage statistics with analytics providers to help us improve the App.

Your financial data (accounts, transactions, ledger entries) is never shared — it never leaves your device except to your own Google Drive when you explicitly enable backup.

🔐 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Because your accounting data is stored locally on your device and backed up only to your personal Google Drive, it benefits from Google's industry-leading security infrastructure when synced.

⚙️ Your Choices

  • You can access or update your Google account information at any time by signing in to your Google account.
  • You can revoke the app's access to Google Drive from your Google account's security settings.
  • If you want to delete your personal information from our records, please contact us. We will take steps to remove it promptly.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's advertising preferences page.
